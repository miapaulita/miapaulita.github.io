    My Mission

    As the President of Women in Computer Science (WiCS) at Florida International University, I am proud to be part of a transformative organization dedicated to advancing women in technology. At WiCS, we strive to bridge the gender gap in tech by fostering an environment of growth, innovation, and inclusivity.

    My mission was clear: to empower women through professional development, networking opportunities, and impactful events such as the Women in Technology Conference (WiTCON). With a vibrant community of over 120 members, including students from FIU and other local colleges, we are committed to providing resources that prepare our members for success in the tech industry.

    Explore my portfolio to see how my experience with WiCS has shaped my skills and achievements. From leading initiatives to connecting with industry professionals, I am excited to share my journey and the impact we are making together.
